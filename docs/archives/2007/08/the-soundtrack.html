The soundtrack to the past 30 days

by phil on Monday Aug 13, 2007 9:38 PM

This is all I've been listening to lately. It's a mixed CD from Marty a.k.a. MC Stank Booty titled "On Some 2006 Shit." I'm not good with using labels, so I won't try to miscategorize it as underground, hipster, eclectic, or whatever. It's just awesome, one of the best compilations I've encountered. The other great compilation that comes to mind is the set of Grand Theft Auto: Vice City soundtracks for each in-game radio station.
