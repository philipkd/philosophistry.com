Philosophistric Recap: Act Naturally

by phil on Wednesday Nov 17, 2010 1:30 PM

I need to think of a way to become less of an over-thinker.

This whole negativity toward Big Government probably has to do with the Post Office and the DMV. That's what everybody thinks when they think "Government." They think, "How could the guys at the Post Office or DMV get anything done."

What if my only purpose in life is to not be the jack-ass who commits a hit-and-run.

At the highest level, success is all luck. Even if you subscribe to Seneca's "luck is what happens when preparation meets opportunity," it's luck that makes you encounter that quote, and it's luck that makes you convert that quote into motivation.
