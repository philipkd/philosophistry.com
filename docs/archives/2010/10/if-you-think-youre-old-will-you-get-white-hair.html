A video gamer's 6th sense

Sometimes I play video games so much I get what is called the Tetris Effect. For example, when I'm parking a car, I feel like I'm fitting in pieces onto the screen. There must be some sort of 6th sense, (or 12th sense depending on how you count them), where you can "feel" the shape of your environment. When parallel parking, you somehow feel a block wedging in there.
