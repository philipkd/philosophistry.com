What happens when you look into the eyes of Google?

by phil on Saturday May 8, 2010 12:20 PM
futureisnow

I was walking home the other day and I saw the Google StreetView car drive right past me. Tickled by the odds of seeing one of those, I tweeted about it. To which, I received a reply, "Guess what, the StreetView for that location will now have a picture of you there for a very long time."

So perhaps seeing a Google StreetView car is like looking into the eyes of Medusa. One glance, and before you're even aware of it, you've become frozen in time, in the Cloud, for everyone to see.
