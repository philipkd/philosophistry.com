New Project: Read On Wiki

by phil on Monday Nov 21, 2011 4:39 PM

I've been really interested in personal information visualization. Heck, that's a big aspect of this blog, with the colored timeline that I have on the home page. I've expanded on this idea on a new project I'm cooking up called Read on Wiki.

Read on Wiki lets you create timelines of your browsing history on wikipedia. The tagline is, "You can tell a lot about a person by what they [Read on Wiki]pedia."
