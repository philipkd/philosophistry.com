Treadmills of Desire and Personal Anguish

by phil on Thursday Mar 2, 2006 7:02 PM

Wherever there is a tremendous personal sacrifice, you will find the perception of an incredible carrot on a stick.

I often find myself burning myself in the process of self-analysis... but when I step back, and ask myself "what for?" I find that there is some pie-in-the-sky beckoning me.

I think I need to be more aware of these various treadmills I place myself on.
