Friends who are doing things that I should be doing . . .

by phil on Monday May 8, 2006 9:27 PM

Friends who are doing things that I should be doing ...
... are awesome.

Ian Spiro, of Hipster T-Shirt Generator fame, contributed to this new infoviz project titled We Feel Fine. It's a collaboration with Sepandar Kamvar and Jonathan Harris, a web artist known for making interesting visual representations of data and imagery.

We Feel Fine represents, with little dots, statements in the blogosphere that begin "I feel ___"

The concept is interesting and wonderfully executed.
