Screenshots of my first architectural project in Second Life

by phil on Thursday May 18, 2006 11:42 PM

Second Life is a collaborative 3D world that is teeming with energy.

I decided to throw my hat in the ring with my first architectural project. Here, I took an image of the proposed World Trade Center memorial and raised it onto a flat monolith. The piece, while simple, touches on 2001 Space Odyssey and modern sculptural trends.
