spotting characteristics at an early age

by phil on Saturday Jan 28, 2006 7:45 PM

my cousin, she's like 6 years old,

she was playing a game with my other cousins, and she was losing, and so she started crying.

her parents were looking on her a little sad like, "maan, why does our daughter keep crying"

then I made the comment, "she's going to be real ambitious some day"

and they said, "why?"

then I said, "cuz, when I was young, I always hated losing, and would behave the same way"

the parents then looked onto their child with great hope.
