Latest Second Life Sculpture: Try Hard Not to Commit Suicide

by phil on Thursday Jun 1, 2006 2:02 AM

Try Hard Not To Commit Suicide is based on a dream where I was a small visitor to a gigantic, multi-story mall that had a looming and pyramidical red staircase in the center. Standing on the staircase, all I could think about was falling off into the materialist abyss. Try Hard is a reinterpretation of that dream in 3D. The pink rails were added to give visitors vertigo. While I was building it, a couple random users walked up it and off the edge, falling off to nowhere.

Note: The meaning of the title is only within the context of the artwork.
