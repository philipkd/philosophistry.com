Idle Thoughts on Habitual Conjunctivitis

by phil on Wednesday Jul 14, 2004 12:43 AM
word-play

Habitual Conjunctivitis is the disease that occurs when two words become habitually conjuncted together by a conjunction, like "and" or "or."

Here are some ones with "and" that Peter and I came up with:

dazed and confused
fear and loathing
crash and burn
intents and purposes
yin and yang
loud and clear
cease and desist
law and order
seek and destroy
clear and present
ready and willing
command and control

dazed and confused? Why not mystified and confused? Is it a poetic stability thing, where the rhyming patterns of the pair are so happy that your tongue and mind can't help itself?
