Harajuku pictures

by phil on Monday Aug 22, 2005 4:59 AM

Harajuku and Omotesando are the trendiest areas of Tokyo (Read the wikipedia entry on Harajuku or check out some fashion pix, both mild and spicy).

I've logged a lot of hours in Harajuku. It was the first place I went to on my first full day in Tokyo. Over the weeks I've become accustomed to the streets that criss-cross all over.

Today I went on a photoblogging frenzy. I almost ran out of battery and space on my digicam.

Here is the damage done.

Harajuku Pix (69 images hosted on flickr)

Harajuku and pictures from Omotesando and my gaijin house (173 images hosted on Yahoo)

Flash slideshow of my favorites (28 pictures)

That's three different services folks. Organizing my media takes some time. I'm getting faster at these things though, using tools like Picasa, Flickr, Yahoo Photos and Flash.

But remember, even only ten years ago we shared photos the old-fashioned way, with little packets of paper holding together glossy hand-held photos that were picked up after being dropped off at a store.
