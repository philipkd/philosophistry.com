The Best Self-Help Books

by phil on Wednesday Feb 4, 2009 12:41 PM
personal projects, self-help_old

The idea of self-help sounds silly to most people, and for good reason. Most self-help just gives you the sensation of inspiration.

Having said that, there are a handful of books that will create authentic, long-lasting change in your life. I have compiled a short list of the ones that have truly changed mine. These books are giants in their domain. I have added this list to the menu of this site and titled it "Bookstore." I get a little commission on the sale of each book, so it's a way to support this site.
