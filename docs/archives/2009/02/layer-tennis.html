Artist Jasper Johns was asked to describe what's involved in the creative process:

It's simple, you just take something and then you do something to it, and then you do something else to it. Keep doing this and pretty soon you've got something.

That's the principle of Layer Tennis. Players pass a Photoshop file back and forth, leaving it transformed forward a little bit each time.

What follows is a little flash tribute to Layer Tennis. I always wanted each volley to blend into the other.
