"Do something cool with your computer"

by phil on Tuesday Jun 17, 2008 8:53 PM

My cousin got a hold of a new computer and he asked me over chat, "So, what's something cool I can do with this?" I didn't know what to say. I said download a web browser and surf the web.

But just now, I saw something that I think perfectly answers that question: Download the Spore Creature Creator Tool (for either Mac or PC). That's the best expression of multimedia, 3D, ease-of-use, do-it-yourself computer ethos in one small package.
