UI Annoyances: "Enlarge" buttons that shrink

by phil on Saturday Sep 13, 2008 3:48 AM

Noticed this on an article atthe Seattle Times. There's an image of Steve Jobs holding an iPod Nano. I was curious to see whether he looked skinnier or not, so I clicked on the "enlarge" button to see more, and voila, I got a shrunken version of the photo. Excellent, thanks!
