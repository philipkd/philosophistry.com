Generative Music and the iPhone: Brian Eno, at it again.

by phil on Friday Oct 10, 2008 4:39 AM

Wow, I just now realized that I've been listening to generative music on my iPhone for the last 30 minutes. It truly is soothing. It comes from Bloom, a Brian Eno creation. I told myself, if nobody makes one of these, I should go ahead and do this. Especially given how popular Electro Plankton has been on the Nintendo DS. Check out Bloom if you have an iPhone, or bug someone with one to get it. It's truly zen.
