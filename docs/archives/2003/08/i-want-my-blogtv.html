I want my BlogTV!

by phil on Sunday Aug 10, 2003 6:54 PM

BlogTV... August 18th is their grand opening... basically it's a TV channel on blogging. The website loads up with chimey music (like Polyphonic Spree) and eerily reminds me of an advertisement for "The Truman Show" within the film The Truman Show. What's the big deal about us only having 15 minutes of privacy in the future when it seems like people are becoming more eager to reveal themselves through blogs... or are there divergent populations... could be as I'm consistently astonished by how many of my most-respected technoliterati peers still write off blogs (primarily because they have no interest in blogging themselves, and thus project that they're useless).
