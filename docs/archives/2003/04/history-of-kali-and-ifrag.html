History of Kali and iFrag, by Jay Cotton It began long ago, right here at this same desk...I discovered Doom! I was fascinated! I couldn't get enough...I'd stay after work to play against Vet students in the lab. Then it happened...I discovered tcpsetup. Jake Page (the author) was a god! We struggled for months trying to setup games using tcpsetup and celebrated every time it worked! It was worth the 2 hours it took to get everyone connected! I was maintaining a list of IP numbers and e-mail addresses of all the players. I found a need to organize all the players so we could find games easier.

A trip back to memory lane. This is what A****ism and I used to play when we had 28.8 modems and Command and Conquer. Aah, the good ol' days... when nothing mattered.
