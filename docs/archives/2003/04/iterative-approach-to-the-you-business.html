Iterative approach to the you-business

by phil on Saturday Apr 12, 2003 2:53 PM
business tips

Keep generating ideas of things for you to do so that if you tried to do them all, you'd be booked. Then, try to come up with ten times as many ideas, i.e. your list would only get done if there were ten of you working simultaneously at it. Then, pick the best things to do that you can fit into your schedule, delegate the rest.
