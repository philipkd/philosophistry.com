BuddyZoo BuddyRank is a measure

by phil on Monday Apr 28, 2003 12:03 AM

BuddyZoo BuddyRank is a measure of a person's importance, computed in the same manner that Google uses PageRank to measure webpage importance. BuddyRank is only computed for members who have submitted their buddy list, and is updated daily. A friend of mine at Stanford is ranked top 50 out of 3.5 billion users. Sorry, make that 3.5 million users.
