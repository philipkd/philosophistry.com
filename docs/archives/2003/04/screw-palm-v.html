Screw Palm V

by phil on Wednesday Apr 16, 2003 6:31 PM

Ditch your Palm V organizing system. My system is better. To remember dates, I create a new word document, arrange it in Landscape mode, and then type in size 72 font the date, time, and name of the event that must be attended to. I then print 3 copies of it. When it's done, I throw these copies all over the floor and in front of my door.

STOP

Theoretically, I'll see these papers often and I won't forget. Also, the act of printing them out in such a big font and then making a scene of it should create a stronger memory association

OR

It could get me duped. Because right where I wrote the first "STOP" above is when my printer started jamming. I had to rip the paper out and got ink all over my hand. I then had to wash it off before I could return and finish the rest. This is my first time doing this, and I think it'll be the last. Either way, it's extremely ironic, and my Palm V is in some way laughing at me. Damn.
