Army and M.I.T. Unveil Futuristic

by phil on Friday May 23, 2003 11:55 AM

Army and M.I.T. Unveil Futuristic Soldier Center DuPont Co. (DD.N), for example, will explore creating light-weight uniforms that change colors on command to camouflage soldiers in changing environments. Other ideas include weaving radio communication materials directly into a uniform's fabric or creating a fuel cell the size of a transistor radio.
