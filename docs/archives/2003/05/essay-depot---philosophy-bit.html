Essay Depot - Philosophy, bit on Heidegger When we witness catastrophe and destruction we tend to blame it on a greater technological framework. But posing a solution to the problem can also be technological. Heidegger sees the treat as an ontological condition from which cannot be saved. He wants to avoid calculative thinking as the only adopted and practiced way of thinking (Dreyfus 101-102). “The danger is a leveling of our understanding of being (99).”
