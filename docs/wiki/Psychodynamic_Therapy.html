Psychodynamic Therapy

Homogeneity is frequently cited as a challenge in conducting systematic reviews of psychodynamic therapy. There are too many treatments and conditions. As Fogany spelled out below, the following conditions are common targets for psychodynamic therapy:

  • Depression
  • Anxiety
  • Eating disorders
  • Somatic problems
  • Drug dependence
  • Psychosis

Example systematic reviews

  • Christiane Steinert (May 2017): Psychodynamic Therapy: As Efficacious as Other Empirically Supported Treatments? A Meta-Analysis Testing Equivalence of Outcomes
