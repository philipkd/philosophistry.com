Mixed improvements

Mixed Impact of Communication on Democracy

There is an aspect of democracy that is like investing, where you're a winner if six out of ten trades are good. Every supposed improvement to democracy has ultimately had slightly-better-than-mixed results.

Demagogic vote segmentation

In the U.S., every odd-numbered class is pitted against the evens: middle-class against the 1%, lower-class whites against blacks.

Tyranny of the majority

Racism is precisely the tyranny of the majority that the Founders feared would happen in a democracy.
